# Examples

Following are examples created with `automesh`.  [Unit Tests](examples/unit_tests/unit_tests.md)
and [Spheres](examples/spheres/spheres.md) illustrate the segmentation and
meshing basics on small, hand-built cases.  The remaining pages are worked
examples for specific commands: [Blobs](examples/defeature/blobs.md) for
`defeature`; a [unit sphere](examples/remesh/sphere.md) and the
[Stanford bunny](examples/remesh/bunny.md) for `remesh`; and
[Laplace](examples/smoothing/laplace.md),
[Laplace with hierarchical control](examples/smoothing/laplace_hierarchical.md),
and [Taubin](examples/smoothing/taubin.md) smoothing, with the
[Python source](examples/smoothing/python_visualization.md) used to generate
the smoothing figures.
